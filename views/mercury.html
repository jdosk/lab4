Mercury

Mercury is the first planet in the solar system. It is the smallest planet, slightly larger than Earth's moon. It's proximity to the sun means it sees extreme temperatures: upwards of 800F during the day, and -180F at night. Mercury has an orbit of about 88 Earth days. Mercury has very little atmosphere, so as a result meteors are not broken up upon entering its atmosphere leading to a crater covered surface.
